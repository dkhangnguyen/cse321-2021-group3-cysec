Description

Our project is making a school club website that works like a forum.
Users can sign up an account to become Club Members, so that they can participate in polling, voting activities posted by Club Officers, as well as posting and commenting on Dicussion Thread post by other member of the club. From Club Member position, user can also apply to become Club Offical, a higher role where they can manage the website.
